# HookScore Google Play Listing

## App Name

HookScore

## Short Description

AI hook scores for short-form videos before you post.

## Full Description

HookScore helps short-form video creators check the opening seconds before publishing.

Paste your first line, describe the video, choose your goal, and get a practical AI-powered hook score with diagnosis, observations, and rewrite suggestions.

Use HookScore to:

- Score short-form video hooks before editing or posting
- Check clarity, specificity, payoff speed, curiosity, audience fit, visual-text match, and scroll resistance
- Add optional visual context from the opening seconds of a video
- Dictate hook context with speech recognition
- Save hook history when signed in
- Try a guest hook check before creating an account
- Report AI-generated results for review
- Delete your account from Settings

HookScore is built for creators, marketers, founders, and teams who want stronger openings for Reels, Shorts, TikToks, ads, and social video concepts.

The app does not promise viral results or platform performance. It gives structured feedback so you can make a sharper first impression before you publish.
